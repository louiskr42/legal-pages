Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how our applications and services collect, use, and handle your information when you use our integrations with third-party platforms such as TikTok.

2. Information We Collect

When you authorize our application to access your TikTok account, we may collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Username, display name, avatar, and unique user identifier (open_id).
  • Content: Videos and photos you choose to publish through our application.
  • Access Tokens: OAuth tokens required to facilitate posting on your behalf. These are stored encrypted and never shared with third parties.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and manage your session
  • To publish content to TikTok as authorized by you
  • To monitor and improve our services
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Data Storage and Security

We store your access tokens and account information on our secure servers. We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption at rest and in transit. Access tokens are automatically refreshed and can be revoked by you at any time through TikTok's account settings.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share data with:

  • TikTok: As required for the API functionality you authorize
  • Service Providers: Third-party infrastructure providers (hosting, database) who are bound by data processing agreements
  • Legal Authorities: When required by applicable law

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time (by disconnecting our app in TikTok settings)
  • Data portability

7. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services. Access tokens are retained until you revoke authorization. Log data is retained for up to 90 days.
